About Tradewind

Maui's trusted auto body shop since 2007.

Est. 2007

Island Pride in Every Panel

Danny and Lehua Souza opened Tradewind Auto Body on Hana Highway in 2007 with one simple goal: give Maui drivers a shop they could actually trust. No games, no hidden charges, no sloppy work. Just honest, professional auto body repair done right the first time.

Over 18 years and 4,000+ vehicles later, we're proud to be the shop that locals recommend to everyone they know. We've seen every type of damage Hawaii's roads and weather can cause — from salt-air rust to storm debris to fender benders in packed parking lots.

Our team of certified technicians takes pride in quality that holds up in Maui's demanding climate. Every repair comes with our written workmanship guarantee.
